Robert Elkjer

Elkjer Music Publishing | 144 Bryce Canyon Road, San Rafael, CA 94903

Robert Elkjer is a music composer, specializing in brass music and piano music. He studied trumpet with Joseph Alessi Sr. and Eugene Blee (Cincinnati Conservatory), and composition with William Thomas McKinley (New England Conservatory). Mr. Elkjer resides in San Rafael, California.
